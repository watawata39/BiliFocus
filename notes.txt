Clean Search right-nav dropdown compromise

Reproduce the problem:
- Open a brand new bilibili.com page in clean search mode (BiliFocus version 2.1.0 or 2.1.1), and hover the cursor on a right-navi item. There may be a flash: the dropdown appears in the unaltered position, partly out of the window, and is then moved to an appropriate position.

Bilibili plays its own fade/drop intro animation when those panels appear; hiding the panel until after the clamp calculation removes the overflow flash, but it also cuts into Bilibili's animation and looks worse.

Current compromise:
- Do not attempt to solve the initial opening flash for now.
- Introduce cache system so the flash is only seen for the first time
